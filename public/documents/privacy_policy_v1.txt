1. Personal information we collect
We collect personal information in two ways:
- You provide the information to inputs on the website
- When you sign into the website using Riot Sign On ("RSO"), or from other third parties (See 1.1)

1.1 Data provided by third-parties
When you sign in to the website using RSO, Riot Games provides the website with information about your account, such as your username and email.

1.2 Data we collect
When you sign in to the website, we keep a record of your Riot account identifier and an email contact. We also use information from your VALORANT account, including your matches, for use in VCT Tools apps ("Apps").

2. Data we disclose
We will never disclose your personal information to the public, however, data such as VALORANT matches will be able to be seen by others through apps if you accept the policy.

2.1 Data requests
We will return you a copy of all personal information we have collected if you wish to view it.

3. Security Measures
Your data requires your authorization to access. Your data is secured in transit using the HTTPS protocol, and sensitive information is hashed or encrypted.

4. Data Retention
User data is kept for the duration of your VCT Tools account (created when you log in with RSO) lifetime. If you request an account deletion from the website, your data will be deleted alongside your account, immediately.

5. User Rights
5.1 Data removal
To delete collected data, you are required to delete your VCT Tools account (See Data Retention).

5.2 Opt Out
We only collect data that is completely necessary for the website to function, and it is not possible to opt out of data collection.

6. Updates
This policy is subject to change. In the case that the policy does change, an email notification will be sent to your email contact that is collected when your account is made. A notice will also be available on the website.

7. Contact Information
You can email "contact@vcttools.net" for questions or concerns regarding the Policy.
